Mulan

Genre: Animation, Kids and Family
Release Date: June 5th, 1998
Director/s: Tony Bancroft, Barry Cook
Production Company: Walt Disney

Movie Summary

When her sick father is drafted by the Chinese Army to fight against the Huns, brave Fa Mulan (Ming Na Wen) decides she must disguise herself as a man to save him from what is almost certain death. Along the way, she picks up many friends like Mushu (Eddie Murphy) the diminutive Dragon and her trusty crew, Ling (Gedde Wantanabe), Yao (Harvey Fierstien) and Chien-Po (Jerry Tondo) that help Mulan in her struggle for not only her family, but for all of China.
